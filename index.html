🏃 Gait Analyzer prototype

Side-view running video → pose estimation → metrics + flags. Everything runs in your browser; the video never leaves your device.
Recording protocol (accuracy depends on this): treadmill preferred · phone on stable surface, side-on, lens at hip height, 3–5 m away · whole body in frame, feet never cropped · 60 fps if possible · 15–30 s steady pace · good lighting, fitted clothing.
